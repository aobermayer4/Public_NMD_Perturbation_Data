- Sapanisertib
	- ATP-competitive dual inhibitor of mTORC1 and mTORC2 pathways

	- 2nd gen ATP-competitive inhibitor of mTORC1 and mTORC2

	- Potent splicing inhibitor

		- Disrupts the interaction between U2 snRNP and pre-mRNA
	- Naturally occurring polyketide macrolide produced by bacterium Streptomyces platensis
	- Impede processing of pre-mRNA -> antitumor activity -> induces apoptosis in some cancer cell lines
